About the Blogger

My name is Rocio Sanchez. I'm a 21 year old student and marketer from Queens, New York. I was born in NYC but I lived a lot of my life in Dominican Republic; my parents immigrated from there in the 80s.

Now? I'm a sixth semester junior at the Fashion Institute of Technology. I study Advertising and Marketing Communications and minor in French. I anticipate graduating with my bachelor's degree in the winter of 2017. I'm entertaining the idea of getting my master's degree in Paris at L'École Superieure de Publicité, but I'll seriously consider that for a while. Money doesn't grow on trees, you know.

This blog is about the jewelry industry. I worked with a small jewelry company through the summer and found the industry very fascinating. This blog is a way for me to explore that!

But jewelry isn't really my niche. I'm actually really involved in the LGBT community! I have a project called "The LGBT Masterlist Project". As a member of the community I felt a personal responsibility to use my professional skills as a marketer and bring valuable content to my followers on my personal Tumblr blog.

At the end of the project, I made a video to accompany the project. The point of the video was to add substance to my content, a face behind a project. It really helped get more followers behind me too!
